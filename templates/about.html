About Prottasha Model School

Discover our journey of educational excellence, our mission to nurture young minds, and our commitment to building a brighter future for our students.


Our History

Prottasha Model School was established in 2010 with a vision to provide quality education to the students of Fatulla, Narayanganj. Founded by a group of dedicated educators and community leaders, our school began its journey with just 50 students and has grown to become one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

Over the years, we have consistently maintained our commitment to academic excellence while fostering character development and moral values. Our school has produced numerous successful graduates who have gone on to excel in various fields, making significant contributions to society.

Today, Prottasha Model School stands as a beacon of educational excellence, serving over 1000+ students from Class one to Class Ten, with a dedicated team of experienced teachers and modern facilities that support comprehensive learning.
