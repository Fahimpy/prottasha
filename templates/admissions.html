Admissions 2025

Join Prottasha Model School and embark on a journey of academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning.

Admission Process

1

Application Collection

Collect application form from school office or download from our website. Forms are available from November 1st.

2

Submit Application

Submit completed application with required documents and application fee by the deadline.

3

Admission Test

Appear for the admission test on the scheduled date. Test covers basic subjects and general knowledge.

4

Result & Admission

Check results and complete admission formalities including fee payment and document verification.

Admission Requirements

Class VI Admission

  • Completed Class V from recognized school
  • Age: 10-12 years (as of December 31, 2024)
  • Satisfactory academic record
  • Pass admission test

Class VII-IX Admission

  • Completed previous class with good grades
  • Transfer certificate from previous school
  • Academic transcript
  • Pass admission test and interview

Class X Admission

  • Completed Class IX with minimum GPA 3.5
  • Strong academic background
  • Character certificate
  • Limited seats available

Required Documents

For All Classes

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original + 2 copies)
  • 4 copies of recent passport-size photographs
  • Application fee receipt
  • Medical certificate

Academic Documents

  • Mark sheet/certificate of last passed class
  • Academic transcript (if applicable)
  • Transfer certificate (for Classes VII-X)
  • Character certificate from previous school
  • Testimonial from previous school

Guardian Information

  • Guardian's NID card copy
  • Guardian's photograph
  • Income certificate (if applicable)
  • Contact information verification
