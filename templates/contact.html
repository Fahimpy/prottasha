Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school timings?

+

School hours are from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM, Sunday through Thursday. Friday and Saturday are weekends.

How can I apply for admission?

+

You can collect the admission form from our school office or download it from our website. Submit the completed form with required documents and fees by the deadline.

Do you provide transportation?

+

Yes, we provide school bus services to various areas in Narayanganj. Please contact our office for route details and fees.

What is the medium of instruction?

+

The medium of instruction is Bangla, following the national curriculum. English is taught as a compulsory subject with emphasis on communication skills.
